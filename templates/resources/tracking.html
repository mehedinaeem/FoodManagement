How Recommendations Work

Our tracking system uses simple, transparent rules to recommend resources:

  • Category Matching: If you log or have inventory items in a category (e.g., dairy), we recommend relevant resource categories (e.g., storage tips, waste reduction).
  • Pattern Detection: If you have items expiring soon, we recommend preservation and waste reduction resources.
  • Activity-Based: Based on your logging frequency and inventory size, we suggest meal planning and budget tips.
  • Transparent: Each recommendation includes an explanation of why it was suggested.
